Welcome to Sally's Mobile Chiropody Clinic!

Sally is a certified chiropodist that takes her mobile clinic on the road! She can bring all the tools to you and give you the treatments you need in the comfort of your own home. I will help to keep your feet in the best of health, making it easier to maintain your active lifestyle and carry on with your day-to-day life.





Treatments

Sally can treat a variety of foot problems, including in-growing toenails, small leg ulcers, verrucae, and fungal nails. A course of 3-4 treatments is required, after which a healthy nail will re-grow and any verrucae will be gone for good.
